Who is Bong Dental Service?

Welcome to Bong Dental Service, a dental clinic established in 2021. We focus on the health and well-being of every patient, offering family-friendly care that ensures each member of your family receives affordable, high-quality dental treatments in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our clinic is equipped with the latest technologies and well-maintained facilities, allowing us to provide excellent service by staying current with the latest advancements in dentistry.


Our team of highly trained dentists brings extensive experience to each patient visit. You can trust that they are dedicated to delivering the best possible care, using the latest techniques and treatments to help you achieve and maintain a healthy, beautiful smile. Thank you for choosing Bong Dental Service. We look forward to serving you and your family.

Frequently Ask Question

How can I schedule an appointment?

Scheduling an appointment at Bong Dental Clinic is easy! You can call us directly at (+63)999 563 3412, or you can book online through our website. If you prefer, you can also email us at bongdental@gmail.com with your preferred date and time, and our staff will get back to you promptly to confirm your appointment.

How should I prepare for my first visit?

To prepare for your first visit to Bong Dental Clinic, please arrive a few minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork. Bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and a list of any medications you are currently taking. If you have previous dental records or X-rays, please bring those as well. It's also helpful to have a list of any questions or concerns you might want to discuss with your dentist.
