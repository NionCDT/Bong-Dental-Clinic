FAQS

Frequently Ask Questions

Our clinic is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

If your insurance is not listed, please contact our office to confirm if we accept your specific plan.

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard) and checks.

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, your dentist may suggest a different frequency based on your individual oral health needs.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office at [09995633412], or by using our online booking system available on our website. New patients can also fill out a registration form online to expedite the process.

For your first visit, please bring your insurance card, a valid ID, and any relevant medical records. If you have completed our new patient forms online, please bring them with you. Arriving 15 minutes early will allow us to complete your registration.

To maintain good oral hygiene, brush your teeth at least twice a day with fluoride toothpaste, floss daily, and use an antiseptic mouthwash. Additionally, maintain a balanced diet and avoid sugary snacks. Regular dental check-ups are also crucial for maintaining oral health.

We understand that schedules can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please provide at least 24 hours' notice. This allows us to accommodate other patients who may need an appointment.

Yes, we offer second opinions. If you have been recommended a treatment plan by another dentist and would like a second opinion, please bring any relevant X-rays and records to your appointment with us.

For after-hours emergencies, please call our emergency hotline at [09995633412]. For non-urgent matters, send an email, and we will respond on the next business day.
